Design rationale

Grounded in real usage

Every mockup was built against a 31-flow audit of the live product. The critical ten — from first visit to GitHub push — are all interactive and reachable in two clicks or fewer.

Three mental models

Rather than proposing one "right" layout, these three directions test whether the product works best as a focused canvas, a multi-project dashboard, or an all-in-one surface.

Responsive from the start

Each direction was designed at 360px, 768px, 1024px, and 1280px simultaneously. Every action is reachable on mobile — panels collapse into sheets, grids stack, and text reflows.
