WordPress Playground UX Redesign

playground.wordpress.net packs powerful features behind a cluttered interface. Too many icon-only buttons, technical settings front-and-center, and no clear starting point for newcomers. Below are three distinct design directions that explore different ways to fix these friction points.

✎
Direction 1

Progressive Disclosure

One big "Launch WordPress" button. Technical settings are tucked into expandable accordion sections that reveal themselves only when you need them.

⌘
Direction 2

Command Palette

Full-bleed WordPress preview with a floating mini-toolbar. Press Ctrl/Cmd+K to open a searchable command palette that replaces menus and modals.

Open mockup →
▦
Direction 3

Dashboard with Cards

A home screen showing your sites as cards alongside starter templates. Click a card to open its WordPress instance. Manage everything from the dashboard.
